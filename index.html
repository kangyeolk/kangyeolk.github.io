About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student at Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), working on machine learning and its applications under the supervision of Jaegul Choo. My main research interests summarize as

- Understanding and manipulating visual information such as images and videos
- Learning structure of objects or scenes from images and videos
- Analyzing and detecting of tampered information made by AI

So far, I have researched on generative modeling for predicting, generating and understanding vision dataset. Also, I have an interest in deploying such research for real-world applications.
